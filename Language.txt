There are 5 languages ​​in the world:

1 — "General", spoken by almost all races, it was invented for communication between kingdoms.

2 — "Parian", spoken by humans.

3 — "Maur", spoken by beastmen.

4 — "Elven", spoken by elves.

5 — "Zorgo", spoken by dwarves.

There are also different monster languages, but no one understands them.
