There are four kingdoms, three of which are on the continent of Troens:

Marco - the kingdom of the beastmen (south of the continent), столица AlKairr.
Zekko - the kingdom of the dwarves (west), столица Kaine .
Maonna - the kingdom of the elves (north), столица Eron.
Eastern Union - the kingdom of humans, located on the Eastern Island, столица Tsukikage.
