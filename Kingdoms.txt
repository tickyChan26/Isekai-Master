There are four kingdoms, three of which are on the continent of Troens:

Marco - the kingdom of the beastmen (south of the continent), capital AlKairr.
Zekko - the kingdom of the dwarves (west), capital Kaine .
Maonna - the kingdom of the elves (north), capital Eron.
Eastern Union - the kingdom of humans, located on the Eastern Island, capital Tsukikage.
