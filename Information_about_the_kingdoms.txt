Elves - are famous for their unity with nature. Some of them can summon familiars in the form of fairies.

Dwarves - are known for their crafting skills and rich mines.

Beastmen - are masters of survival, their settlements are located in arid lands and jungles.

Humans - have achieved superiority in shipbuilding, which gives them an advantage on the water and allows them to trade with the kingdom of Zekko.
